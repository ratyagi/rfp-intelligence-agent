# Commercial Terms Register — Milestone Payment Structures

**Owner:** Commercial Director
**Purpose:** Record of milestone-based payment structures agreed in prior
engagements, for reuse in proposals.

## Standard milestone payment model (as agreed in prior contracts)

| Milestone | Trigger | Typical % of contract value |
|---|---|---|
| M1 — Mobilisation | Project initiation and plan approval | 10% |
| M2 — Landing zone accepted | Client signoff of platform build | 15% |
| M3 — Per migration wave | Wave completion certified by client | 50% (pro-rated) |
| M4 — Datacentre exit / go-live | Final workload cutover accepted | 15% |
| M5 — Hypercare exit | End of stabilisation period | 10% |

## Established terms

- **Milestone completion certified by the client's nominated project
  director** before invoicing — used on HealthGov (DOC-001), StateGov
  (DOC-002), and FinanceDept (DOC-003) engagements
- **Net-30 payment terms** from receipt of correctly rendered invoice —
  our standard across all government contracts since 2022
- No advance payments beyond the mobilisation milestone
- Liquidated damages and holdback provisions accepted where required by
  Commonwealth contracting frameworks

## Invoicing history

Across the last 8 government engagements: zero disputed milestone
certifications; average payment cycle 24 days.
