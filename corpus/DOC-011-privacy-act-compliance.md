# Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) Compliance Statement

**Owner:** General Counsel / Privacy Officer
**Version:** 2.4 (May 2025)

## Compliance posture

- Contoso Cloud Solutions operates a privacy management framework aligned
  to the **Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)** and the Australian Privacy Principles
  (APPs), reviewed annually by external counsel.
- A **Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)** is completed for every
  engagement that involves personal information, before any data migration
  activity begins. (Most recent precedent: Project Aurora DPIA, May 2025,
  confirmed by Legal as fully compliant.)
- All personnel complete annual privacy training; delivery teams handling
  government data complete additional ISM-aligned modules.

## Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme procedure

- We maintain a documented data breach response plan tested via annual
  tabletop exercise (last exercised March 2025).
- **Client notification SLA: within 24 hours of discovery** of any eligible
  or suspected data breach affecting client data — contractually committed
  in our standard government terms and met on the single notifiable event
  in company history (2021; contained, assessed, no client data exfiltrated).
- Statutory OAIC notification obligations are managed jointly with the
  client in accordance with the NDB scheme.

## Contractual position

We accept breach-notification clauses of 24 hours or longer without
amendment, and indemnity positions consistent with Commonwealth contracting
frameworks.
