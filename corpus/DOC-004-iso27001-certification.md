# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Certification — Summary of Currency and Scope

**Certificate holder:** Contoso Cloud Solutions Pty Ltd
**Certificate number:** AU-27001-48213 (fictional)
**Certification body:** Southern Cross Assurance Services (fictional)
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022
**Initial certification:** March 2019 (under ISO/IEC 27001:2013, transitioned 2024)
**Current certificate valid:** 1 March 2025 – 31 March 2027
**Last surveillance audit:** February 2026 — zero non-conformities

## Certified scope

The information security management system (ISMS) covering:

- Cloud migration, managed services, and platform operations delivery
- All client data handling, storage, and processing activities
- Supporting corporate functions (HR, commercial, service desk)
- All Australian delivery locations (Sydney, Melbourne, Canberra)

## Relevance to government procurement

- The ISMS scope explicitly includes **all cloud delivery operations and
  client data handling practices** — no carve-outs.
- Statement of Applicability covers all Annex A controls; 3 controls are
  excluded with documented justification (physical controls for premises
  we do not operate).
- Certificate and audit reports are available to clients under NDA on request.
