# Executed SOW Extract — Fixed-Price Pricing Schedule

**Engagement:** ClientCo Enterprises — Cloud Migration and Managed Services
**Status:** Executed 22 July 2025 (signature pages held in contract register)
**Extract purpose:** Internal evidence of accepted fixed-price commercial
structure for reuse in government proposals. Commercially sensitive figures
redacted; structure and terms shown as agreed.

## Agreed pricing structure

- **Fixed-price schedule across Years 1, 2, and 3**, broken down by
  migration delivery (Year 1) and managed services (Years 1–3)
- **Annual escalation capped at CPI + 2%**, applied only from Year 2, using
  the ABS All Groups CPI (Australia) figure for the preceding June quarter
- Pricing is **all-inclusive**: software licences, migration tooling
  (Azure Migrate, Azure Site Recovery), professional services, project
  management, and transition costs
- No charge for cutover rehearsals or rollback events

## Client acceptance

ClientCo procurement confirmed in writing (22 July 2025): *"We accepted the
fixed-price Year 1–3 schedule. The CPI+2% escalation cap was agreed and
documented in the signed SOW."*

## Reuse guidance

This structure has been accepted in three subsequent engagements including
FinanceDept Agency (DOC-003, delivered on fixed price with no variations)
and is our standard offer for government multi-year procurements.
